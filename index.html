Hyein Kim

Based in Seoul, South Korea

M.A. Urban Planning and Design | University of Seoul (2025) B.A. English Literature | Sungkyunkwan University (2019)

My research examines how social infrastructure shapes socioeconomic interactions in urban spaces, with a focus on Seoul's Living SOC policy and its implications for social cohesion.

I am and urban explorer who loves observing cities and crafting those experiences into research, writing, and creative works. I amd currently pursuing a Master's degree in Urban Planning and Design at the University of Seoul.

With a background in both the humanities and urban planning, I'm intersected in where the art of cities meets the science of urban data analytics.

My research explores how urban data can be leveraged to understand how people move, encounter, and interact within urban spaces and places.
