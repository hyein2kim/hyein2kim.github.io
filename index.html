I am an urban explorer who loves observing cities and crafting those experiences into research, writing, and creative works. I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Urban Planning and Design at the University of Seoul.

With a background in both the humanities and urban planning, I'm interested in where the art of cities meets the science of urban data analytics.

My research explores how urban data can be leveraged to understand how people move, encounter, and interact within urban spaces and places.
